================================================================================
JUNIPER BIBLE - NIXOS SERVER QUICKSTART
================================================================================

Boot NixOS installer ISO, get network access, then ONE command:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ONE-LINER (interactive - prompts for confirmation and SSH key)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JuniperBible/juniper-server/main/bootstrap.sh | sudo bash

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ONE-LINER (fully automated - no prompts)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JuniperBible/juniper-server/main/bootstrap.sh | sudo bash -s -- /dev/sda "ssh-ed25519 AAAA..."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
AFTER REBOOT - Deploy the site
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ssh deploy@YOUR_SERVER_IP
deploy-juniper

Or from your dev machine:
make vps VPS=deploy@YOUR_SERVER_IP:/var/www/juniperbible
